Chicken Enchilada

Description

Some recipes take a little more time to make but are so worth the effort

This chicken enchilada recipe couldnâ€™t be easier to make with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • A cup of cheese
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Dried herbs
  • Green pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken, slice it into cubes, then return it to the skillet
  2. Add the onion, a cup of cheese, sour cream, and dried herbs.
  3. Melt the cheese. Stir in tomato sauce, green pepper, garlic, chili powder, and salt
  4. Fill the enchiladas and roll them up. Arrange them in a baking dish.
  5. Top with taco sauce and remaining cheese. Bake until the cheese is melted.
  6. In an oven preheated to 350 degrees F, these chicken enchiladas should be perfectly cooked in about 20 minutes. You'll know the enchiladas are done when they are heated through and the cheese is melted.
